# Support

Need help with Castor or want to go further? Here are your options depending on
your needs.

## Community Support

If you have a question, found a bug, or want to suggest an improvement, the best
place to start is our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/jolicode/castor).

- Open a [new issue](https://github.com/jolicode/castor/issues/new/choose)
- Report a bug or unexpected behavior
- Propose an enhancement or discuss new features

The team and community will do their best to help you.

> [!TIP]
> Recommended option if you just need help with your setup or have a specific
> question.

## Professional Training

For teams or developers who want to master Castor in depth,
[JoliCampus](https://jolicampus.com/formations/castor-php-task-runner) offers a
**1-day professional training** dedicated to Castor.

You’ll learn how to:

- Install, configure, and structure your automation scripts
- Write and organize complex tasks
- Build custom extensions for your specific needs
- Integrate Castor with your CI/CD pipelines and other tools
- Apply best practices and real-world workflows from JoliCode’s experience

The course is taught by the team behind Castor, sharing real use cases and
expert tips to help you get the most out of the tool.

[Learn more on JoliCampus](https://jolicampus.com/formations/castor-php-task-runner)

> [!TIP]
> Recommended option if you want to level up your automation skills and get
> expert guidance.
